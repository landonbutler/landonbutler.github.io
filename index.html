Bio

I am a third year Ph.D. student in EECS at Berkeley, advised by Kannan Ramchandran and supported by an NSF Graduate Research Fellowship. Previously, I completed my Master's in Data Science and my Bachelor's in Systems Engineering at the University of Pennsylvania, where I researched under the supervision of Alejandro Ribeiro. My research lies at the intersection of machine learning, signal processing, and game theory, inspired by applications in interpretability of foundation models.
